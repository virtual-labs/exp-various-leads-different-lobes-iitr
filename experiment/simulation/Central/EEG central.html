                   Different leads in Central Lobe.



Central Lobe -
In Central lobe three leads are present i.e C3,Cz & C4 and recording of EEG signals is done in unipolar mode.





STEP 1 : Make all the Correct Connections.

1.  Connect C3 lead point of human brain to the positive terminal (C3) of first op-amp circuit.

2.  Connect Cz lead point of human brain to the positive terminal (Cz) of second op-amp circuit.

3.  Connect C4 lead point of human brain to the positive terminal (C4) of third op-amp circuit.

4.  Connect Ground lead point A1 of human brain to the negative terminal (A1) of op-amp circuit.

5.  Connect Ground lead point A2 of human brain to the negative terminal (A2) of op-amp circuit.

                      

STEP 2 : Click on check button to check for Correct Connections.

STEP 3 : If Connections are not correct click on reset button for reset connections.

STEP 4 : If Connections are correct click on play/pause button to see recorded eeg signals for Fp1 and Fp2.

STEP 5 : Click on waveform tab for data obsevation and measurement (Waveform will only get appear after correct connections).

STEP 6 : Click on print button to save the observations.
